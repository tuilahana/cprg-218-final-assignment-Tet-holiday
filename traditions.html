Tet is filled with meaningful traditions, such as cleaning and decorating homes to welcome the new year. A key practice is ancestor worship, where families pay respects to their ancestors, and the giving of red envelopes with money(lì xì) signifies wishes for good luck and fortune.

To extend our Tet greetings, we often convey our wishes with the words: Happy New Year (Chúc mừng năm mới), may you have prosperity and good health (An khang thịnh vượng - Sức khoẻ dồi dào), and may all your endeavors be fulfilled (Vạn sự như ý).
